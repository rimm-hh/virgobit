About

VirgoBit collaborates with Oldenburg University on an exciting project to develop an app for visualizing event-related potentials (ERPs). The app integrates with the Lab Streaming Layer (LSL) to capture EEG data and event markers. Through epoching, filtering, and averaging techniques, researchers can analyze and display ERPs based on selected markers and EEG channels.

    Goals

  • Integration of Analysis Techniques which enable researchers to extract meaningful information from the raw EEG data and enhance the visualization of ERPs.
  • Integrating with the Lab Streaming Layer (LSL) that allows researchers to identify and analyze specific brain responses associated with stimuli or tasks.
