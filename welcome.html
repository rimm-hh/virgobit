This Cookie Policy explains how we use cookies and similar technologies to recognize you when you visit our website, and to enhance your experience by making our website more user-friendly, convenient, and secure.

What are Cookies:

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your computer or mobile device when you visit our website. They allow us to remember your preferences and help us to analyze how our website is used so we can improve its functionality.

Types of Cookies:

We use several different types of cookies, including:

  • Essential Cookies: These cookies are necessary for our website to function properly and cannot be turned off in our system.

  • Analytical/Performance Cookies: These cookies allow us to track visitor usage of our website and help us to improve the way our website works.

  • Functionality Cookies: These cookies allow us to remember your preferences and personalize your experience on our website.

  • Advertising/Targeting Cookies: These cookies are used to deliver targeted advertisements based on your interests and browsing history.

Managing Cookies:

You can manage your cookie preferences at any time through your browser settings. Please note that disabling cookies may limit your ability to use certain features of our website.
