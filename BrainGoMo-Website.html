About

The BrainGoMo Case Study provides an in-depth analysis of the innovative solution offered by BrainGoMo, a company that aims to promote the use of neurofeedback technology in the field of mental health. The case study focuses on BrainGoMo's products, including a self-designed headset and a developed app, as well as its web server, which provides a dashboard for doctors and patients to access neurofeedback data.

View BrainGoMo Website

    Problem

  • Solving the problem of inadequate awareness and understanding of neurofeedback technology among doctors, patients, and scientists
  • Low engagement of the previous version of the website due to poor user experience and an outdated design

    Goals

  • Introducing BrainGoMo's products to doctors, scientists, and patients and promoting the use of neurofeedback technology as an effective and accessible tool for mental health treatment.
  • Improving user experience and increasing engagement with a trendy and user-friendly website design, as well as clearly demonstrating the benefits and potential of the BrainGoMo neurofeedback headset and app.
